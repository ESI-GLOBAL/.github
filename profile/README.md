# ESI Global

ESI Global builds practical software and operational systems that support professional learning, workforce readiness, and reliable program delivery.

We focus on secure, maintainable products that help teams turn complex requirements into clear learning experiences and repeatable operations.

## How we work

- Security and privacy are built into delivery decisions.
- Changes are reviewed through pull requests and automated validation.
- Documentation and operational readiness are part of the product.
- Reliability, accessibility, and sustainable maintenance take priority over short-term shortcuts.

Most engineering repositories are private. Public materials in this organization are intentionally limited to resources suitable for external use.

## Security

Please do not disclose suspected vulnerabilities in public issues or discussions. Follow our [security policy](../SECURITY.md) to report concerns privately.
